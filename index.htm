What is 6chan?

6chan is a minimalist imageboard platform inspired by the early internet culture of the 2000s. Unlike traditional social media, 6chan emphasizes:

  • Anonymity: Most posts are made without user accounts
  • Ephemerality: Threads eventually expire based on activity
  • Image-focused: Content revolves around shared images/media
  • Community-moderated: Users police content through reports

The platform is organized into different "boards" (like /b/, /a/, /v/) each dedicated to specific topics. There are no profiles, algorithms, or persistent identities - just raw, unfiltered discussion.

6chan operates under three simple rules:

  1. No illegal content
  2. No spam/advertising
  3. No disrupting the user experience

All content is user-generated. The administration takes no responsibility for posts and merely provides the platform as a free service.
